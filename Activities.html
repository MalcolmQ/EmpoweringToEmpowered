Our Programs & Activities

Empowering youth and women through education, creativity, wellness, and community support.

Pastries & Catering

The women and youth learn pastry creation, catering management, and food service skills. They prepare pastries for birthdays, weddings, graduations, and community events while learning sanitation, equipment use, and supply management.

Self Care & Wellness

We teach the importance of mental and physical wellbeing—covering sleep, nutrition, exercise, and emotional health—so youth can build healthy lifelong habits.

Women's Engagement

Women empower one another through mentorship, counseling, and community support. We foster unity, confidence, and a safe space for growth and healing.

Graduation Celebrations

We honor the achievements of our youth with graduation ceremonies that celebrate their growth, skills, and accomplishments in the program.

Business Development

Youth learn entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and how to turn their skills into income‑generating opportunities—building confidence and independence.

Design & Creative Arts

Students explore creativity through crafts, sewing, interior decoration, and design fundamentals—building skills they can use personally and professionally.

Interior Decoration & Crafts

Youth learn event decoration, crafting, and creative design skills to bring beauty and joy to community events.

Sewing & Tailoring

Students learn sewing basics, clothing repair, and tailoring—skills that help them create, customize, and sustain their own garments.
