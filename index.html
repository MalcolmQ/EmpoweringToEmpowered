Elizabeth Youth and Women Empowerment Program Center

What is it that you will see happen when you empower less fortunate youth and women in Liberia with the tools to succeed?

You help build their confidence. You give them hope for a better future. You share the love of God. And You transform their lives.

WHAT WE DO?

Our empowerment program serves the youth (from kindergarten to young adults in college) and women daily. We reach out to the communities in Liberia to continue to increase opportunities for the youth in the community by providing classes/courses that will further build up their skillsets. This will propel them to have better futures, to lead better lives.

Education

Our program bolsters and enhances the education and the skillsets of young liberians. Our cirriculum blends reading, writing, math, technologies and the sciences, with arts and buisness entrepeneurship. We connect what they learn to how it can be practically used in real world settings. So they get to get the full educational and practical experience.

Life Skills

We introduce and teach the youth and women skills which are very valuable in everyday life. From Sewing, entrepeneurship, culinary pastry making, cosmetology, interior decoration, crafts and more. These skills learned will allow them to make a difference and impact in their community.

Community

Through our programs we do our best to positively impact the community in a productive way. From education, lending assistance, providing availability of clinical care to the women and children in the community. We also give back and have social events with food, where people in the community can enjoy a good meal, and have a sense of community, as we prepare the youth to excel and help their communities to grow in a positive direction.
