Elizabeth Youth & Women Empowerment Program

Empowering youth and women in Liberia with education, life skills, and hope for a brighter future.

Support Our Mission

Transforming Lives Through Empowerment

When you empower less fortunate youth and women with the tools to succeed, you build confidence, restore hope, share love, and transform entire communities.

What We Do

We serve youth from kindergarten to college and women across Liberia by providing education, life skills, community support, and access to essential resources that help them build brighter futures.

Our Core Programs

Education

We strengthen academic skills through reading, writing, math, science, technology, and entrepreneurship — connecting learning to real‑world application.

Life Skills

Youth and women learn sewing, culinary arts, cosmetology, interior decoration, entrepreneurship, and more — skills that empower them to thrive.

Community

We support the community through education, clinical care, outreach, and events that bring people together and foster unity.
