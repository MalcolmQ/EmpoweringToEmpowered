Elizabeth Bowens Youth & Women Empowerment Program

Building hope, dignity, and opportunity for youth and women in Liberia.

Our Mission

The mission of the Elizabeth Bowens Youth and Women Center is to instill Godly values among the youth and women of Liberia. We aim to promote spiritual and social growth within the community by providing tools for daily living, empowerment, and hope.

Our History

Established in 2020 and officially opened on March 20, 2021, the Elizabeth Bowens Youth Center stands as a tribute to Mrs. Bowen—a loving, selfless caregiver who impacted countless children in her community. The center continues her legacy by offering programs that uplift, empower, and nurture youth in a safe and supportive environment.

Our Vision

Every empowered youth and woman will become self‑reliant and use the life skills they learn to make a meaningful difference in their community.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: Supporting each youth and woman based on their individual needs.
  • Respect: Treating everyone with dignity, compassion, and fairness.
  • Transparency: Maintaining open stewardship of donated resources.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and consistency in all interactions.
