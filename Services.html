After School Programs & Community Services

Supporting youth and women through education, discipline, health care, and community engagement.

After School Programs

Our youth participate in academic support, skill‑building activities, and social engagement. We also provide recreational time, games, and movie days to reward their hard work and dedication.

General Discipline & Character Building

We help youth develop:

  • Good study habits
  • Persistence and resilience
  • Healthy personal systems and routines
  • Proper etiquette and decorum

Lorpu Lemue Maternal Child Health & Community Medical Clinic

Our clinic provides essential health services including general assessments, prenatal care, labor and delivery support, diagnostic evaluations, and counseling for mothers and children.

Services include monitoring vitals, physical examinations, diagnostic testing, and providing guidance based on assessment results.

You can contact us to learn how to support our clinic’s work.
