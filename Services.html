AFTER SCHOOL Programs

What is happening After School?

The youth are involved in various extracurriculuar activities and studies, to continue to strengthen their academic skillsets and also be involved socially with one another. There are occasions where we have the children involved in recreational fun, games and movies etc for all their hard work, time and devotion they put in to sharpening their skills.

General Discipline

When it comes to our youth we aim for them to build:

  1. 1.Good Study Habits
  2. 2.Persistence
  3. 3.Building Healthy and efficient systems in their lives
  4. 4.Good Ettiquette/Decorum

Lorpue Lemue Maternal Child Health & Community Medical Clinic

The Elizabeth Bowens Youth and Women empowerment program also has a clinic named "Lorpu Lemue Maternal Child Health & Community Medical Clincic" in place that provides general assessments, prenatal care, labor and delivery, diagnosis evaluations, and counseling.

The general assessments includes Monitoring patients vitals, Conducting physical examinations, and performing diagnostic tests. Our clinic provides support for the mothers and the children along with advice to the patients based on their diagnosis/ results of their assessments.

You can contact us to find out ways you can support our clinics work in the community.
